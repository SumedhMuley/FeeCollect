BLUE ACADEMY - FeeCollect App
==============================

A mobile app for managing your sports coaching academy.


WHAT IT DOES
------------
• Track students - names, contact info, batches
• Collect fees - record monthly payments, track pending dues
• Mark attendance - daily presence/absence tracking
• Send reminders - WhatsApp/SMS payment reminders with one tap
• Daily notifications - get reminded about pending payments
• Export data - download reports as CSV files


HOW TO USE
----------
1. Add your batches (e.g., "Morning Batch", "Evening Batch")
2. Add students with their contact details
3. When fee is due, add a fee entry for each student
4. Mark it as "Paid" when they pay
5. Use "Send Reminder" to message students with pending fees


MAIN SCREENS
------------
• Dashboard - Quick overview of collections and pending amounts
• Students - View and manage all students
• Attendance - Mark daily attendance
• Reports - View collection stats and export data
• Settings - Set notification time, academy name, etc.


NOTIFICATIONS
-------------
The app sends you a daily reminder (default: 9:00 AM) if there are 
pending payments. You can change the time in Settings.


DATA
----
All data is stored locally on your phone. Use "Export Data" in 
Settings to backup your data as a CSV file.
